### Key Highlights

#### Addicted to learning like addicted to social media
**Brain Decoding**: The brain often prioritizes social media over learning because social media provides fast rewards (dopamine), triggers curiosity, and consumes very little energy. Conversely, learning requires long-term focus, yields slow results, and does not provide immediate rewards.
**Turn the Brain into a "Dopamine Game"**: The key to motivation is recognizing the value of reward for each learning task. It is necessary to establish a clear reward/punishment mechanism (e.g., creating a lucky draw box after each study session) to build a reward loop.
**3 "Brain Hack" Tips**:
**Leverage Fear for Learning**: Humans tend to fear loss (fear of missing out/losing) more than enjoying gains. Create a "study streak" to generate a fear of breaking the chain.
**Divide Study Time**: Trick the brain's nucleus: Divide study time into 10-minute blocks, repeated multiple times instead of studying continuously for hours.
**Create Feedback from Everyone**: After completing a task, reward yourself with a 10-minute break. Apply social media mechanics to learning, such as building streaks, leaderboards, and creating anticipation for the next event.
**Conclusion**: Winners are not those who always have motivation, but those who know how to turn motivation into a learning habit.

### Why not working yet - Mindset for "AI Ready Freshers"
**Overcoming AI Fear**: The explosion of AI leaves students feeling overwhelmed and fearful of being replaced. The solution is to focus on core values that never change and master 5 foundational services to get started.
**Principles of Using AI**: Use AI but truly understand the core essence. There is no absolute right/wrong, only doing better. Always know how to "question" the results generated by AI. Do not hesitate to seek community support and dare to make mistakes to learn.
**Integrity**: A vital factor in teamwork. Show everyone that you are someone who "sees things through to the end," thereby building solid trust with teammates in the project.
**3 Circles of Work**: Love - Benefits - Have to do.
**5 Work Benefits**: Salary, Experience, Network, Knowledge/Skills, Personal Growth.
**Enterprise Candidate Evaluation Criteria**: Attitude, Aptitude/Qualification, Experience, Life Experience, and Potential.

### Software Development Methodologies & Bmad Framework
**Warning on "Vibe Coding"**: Coding with AI purely by intuition (Vibe coding) is only suitable for demos and cannot create production-ready software. Overuse will cause developers to forget core knowledge.
**The Shift**: Shift mindset from simple "Prompting" (asking basic questions) to "Context Engineering" to provide sufficient data for AI to understand the full system.
**Introduction to Bmad**: A framework that fits perfectly with current software development standards.
**Standardized AI Workflow**:
Assign roles to AI, defining clearly which position each AI "member" assumes.
**Create a Closed Pipeline**: Brainstorming chat -> Project initialization -> When scenarios/data are sufficient, transition to Coder -> Transition to Tester -> Iterate the process until completion.

### Key Learnings
### Motivation Optimization & Study Habits (Brain Hacking)
**Dopamine Mechanism**: Understand how the brain prioritizes short-term rewards to build a clear reward/punishment loop for studying (e.g., lucky draw box, study streak).
**Divide Objectives**: Trick the brain by dividing time into short slots (10-minute blocks) to overcome procrastination.
**Apply Gamification**: Bring social media engagement mechanics (streaks, leaderboards, instant feedback) into the learning process to turn motivation into a habit.

### System Design & Prompting Techniques for AI
**Cost-Optimized Project Architecture**: Master the mindset of interconnecting services to build an operating system with minimal cost while maintaining performance.
**Automated Prompting Techniques**: Thoroughly understand methods for designing and structuring professional, detailed prompts for AI systems.
**Context Engineering Mindset**: Shift from simple prompting to providing comprehensive context and system data so AI can deliver the most accurate responses.

### Standardized Software Development Methods with AI
**Overcoming "Vibe Coding"**: Abandon intuitive coding mindsets (only good for demos) to align with production-ready code standards.
**Applying Bmad Framework**: Adopt a standardized framework that facilitates effective collaboration with AI in modern software development workflows.
